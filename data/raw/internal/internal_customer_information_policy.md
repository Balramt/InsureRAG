# Internal Customer Information Policy

## Section 1: Purpose

This policy defines the minimum customer information standards that product, legal, and compliance teams must review before launching or modifying insurance products.

The purpose is to ensure that customer-facing insurance documents are clear, complete, and aligned with applicable legal, regulatory, and internal requirements.

## Section 2: Scope

This policy applies to insurance products offered by the company in Germany.

It applies to product managers, compliance managers, legal reviewers, distribution teams, and customer communication teams involved in insurance product approval or modification.

## Section 3: Pre-contract Customer Information

Before an insurance contract is concluded, the responsible product team must ensure that customer-facing information is prepared and reviewed.

The customer information package should include:

- product description,
- insured risks,
- main exclusions,
- premium structure,
- contract duration,
- cancellation rights,
- complaint channels,
- customer obligations,
- data protection information,
- key contact points.

## Section 4: Product Manager Responsibilities

The product manager is responsible for preparing the initial customer information package and submitting it for compliance review.

The product manager must ensure that the information is understandable for the intended target group and consistent across product documents, sales material, and digital customer journeys.

## Section 5: Compliance Review

The compliance team must review whether the customer information package is aligned with applicable German insurance law, EU insurance distribution requirements, and internal policy standards.

If a requirement is unclear, the compliance team must escalate the issue to the legal department before product launch.

## Section 6: Evidence and Documentation

The final reviewed customer information package must be stored together with the product approval record.

The record should include the reviewed documents, review date, reviewer name or team, open compliance issues, and final approval status.
